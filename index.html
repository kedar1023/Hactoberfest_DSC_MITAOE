DSC MITAOE HacktoberFest

Support Open Source and earn a limited edition T-shirt

We’re hosting an in-person meetup at MITAOE on 18th October, from 3pm - 5pm, to gather all free and open source enthusiasts from the Pune city to contribute to open source projects together. Hacktoberfest is an annual month-long event to celebrate and encourage more developers to become involved with open source software. It’s open for anyone, especially beginners just getting started with open source! In this meetup, we’ll take a beginner friendly session on Git and GitHub. The most active contributors, who make at least 4 PR’s merged, will receive T-shirts sponsored by Digital Ocean.
